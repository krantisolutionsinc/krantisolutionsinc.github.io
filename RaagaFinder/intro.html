Welcome to Raagafinder.com! We are a music portal dedicated to Carnatic Music, the system of classical music practised in South India. At Raagafinder.com, we aim to enhance the popularity of Carnatic music and make it more accessible to one and all.

Raagafinder is a web application that enables the user to find out the raaga associated with a song. The user plays the song on the music synthesizer that is provided by the application. The application then determines the list of raagas that best match the tune played. Please contact us at support@krantisolutionsinc.com for any questions, feedback or information.
